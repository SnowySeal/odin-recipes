Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Carrot
  • Wide Egg Noodles
  • Shredded Chicken
  • Chicken skin
  • Seasoning (Onion powder, Paprika, Oregano, Italian seasoning, Cajun)
  • Chicken Broth
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Add oil to a pot
  2. Boil wide egg noodles in a seprate pot
  3. Add 2 part onion to 1 part celery to 1 part carrot
  4. Add chicken skin
  5. Add garlic once vegtables are done
  6. Add shredded chicken
  7. Season with onion powder, paprika, oregano, italian seasoning and a bit of cajun
  8. Add Chicken Broth
  9. Serve with wide egg noddles
