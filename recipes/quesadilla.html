Chicken quesadilla

Description

A chicken quesadilla is made up of two totillas with a filling of cheese, chicken and other vegetables. This recipe uses the rotasrie chicken from costco for its relative cheap cost and easy of use. The recipe uses bell peppers and onions for the vegtables.

Ingredients

  • Shredded Chicken
  • Seasoning (paprika, cajun, salt and pepper)
  • Tortilla
  • Bell Peppers
  • Onions
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Seasone chicken with paprika, cajun, salt and pepper
  2. Chop bell peppers and onions
  3. Add chicken, bell peppers, onion and cheese to a tortilla
  4. Add a splash of oil to a pan
  5. Cook the quesadilla and serve
